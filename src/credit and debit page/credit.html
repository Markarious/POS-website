DEBIT & CREDIT CARDS PAYMENT

Pay with a Credit / Debit Card for your orders at ITNET.com.gh with our secured payment checkout. In order to ensure the privacy of your payment, our site is protected with a Premium Extended Validation (EV) Secure Socket Layer (SSL) Certificate. Your card must be activated for online purchases by your bank or issuing financial institution.

We accept cards from the following:

  • MasterCard
  • Visa Credit/Debit Card
  • American Express

To checkout using Debit and Credit Card, kindly follow the steps below:

Step 1: Select “Pay with Card / Mobile Money”

Step 2: Select “Pay with Card”

Step 3: Enter your Card details such as:

  • Card Number: The 16-digit unique number on your card
  • Expiry Date (Valid Till): The expiry date on your card usually written in month and year.
  • Security Code (CVV): The CVV Number ("Card Verification Value") on your credit card or debit card is a 3-digit number on VISA®, MasterCard® and Discover® branded credit and debit cards. On your American Express® branded credit or debit card it is a 4-digit numeric code.

Step 4: Click on ‘PAY’

For security reasons, some banks may ask you to verify your identity by providing your Verified by Visa (VBV) password or your One-Time Password (OTP) to be sent to your email or registered mobile phone number with your bank.

Note: We do not retain your Credit / Debit Card information after your order is completed; it is submitted directly to our banks through our Secure Payment Gateway and verified by our Premium EV SSL Certificate.
